EVENTS

Free your mind and body to indulge in an abundance of events stimulating your sense and kindling the flame of art. An amalgamation of contemporary and traditional events at Adizya lets you feel the aura of true art and design through a display of formal events and lets you lighten yourself with equally challenging informal events that are meant to invigorate and inspire creative minds.

What makes Adizya complete is the plenitude of eminent personalities who have graced our stages. Be it of traditional or contemporary genre, Adizya has stood witness to the best in the fields as they speak of their experiences in carving a better and brighter world. Convening dignitaries from our esteemed alumni to renowned architects such as Ar. Dean D’Cruz and Ar. Vinu Daniel, who honoured us with their inspiring speeches and lectures.

LECTURES

WORKSHOPS

Adizya sets up a wide variety of unique and distinct workshops such as xylography, pottery, jewellery making, photography, pencil art etc. aiming to provide an experience of assisted learning and skill developing, in arenas unexplored by the commons. It brings to you a platform to sharpen cool and formative minds and set out on an artistic expedition.

Explore our state-of-the-art exhibitions showcasing the talents of our students and those unable to express it for themselves. Adizya brings forth a wide range of paintings and sculptures that unfolds glorifying appreciation for a variety of art and design hidden within all of us. Each year aiming to augment our collection with many more innovative creations to amaze each and every one.

EXHIBITIONS

INFORMALS

Our events, lectures and exhibitions are not only what makes Adizya the success that it is. We also host a live continuous informals' stall which is set up to entertain our guests with a variety of games, challenges and impromptu performances, providing a moment to lighten up and loosen yourself in the performances and fun games or maybe push yourself and face some of the challenges and come out victorious. Informals welcomes everyone for a spectacular time at Adizya ’18.
